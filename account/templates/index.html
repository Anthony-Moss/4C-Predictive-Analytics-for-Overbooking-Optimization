Optimizing overbooking.

Hotels use overbooking as a revenue management strategy to optimize RevPAR. While this approach does increase profitability, overbooking can cause problems for travelers and hotels. When a hotel is unable to accommodate a guest due to overbooking, the Front Desk Staff must find a suitable alternative for the guest. Costs include a room at another hotel, meals, transportation, vouchers or other forms of compensation. Walking guests can also be costly in terms of staff overhead.
